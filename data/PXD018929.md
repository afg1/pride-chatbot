### Title
Novel Aza-Podophyllotoxin Derivative in Triple Negative Breast Cancer

### Description
Four cell lines (MCF10A, MCF12A, MDA-MB-231, and MDA-MB-468) were treated with a novel drug candidate.

### Sample Protocol
Cells were treated with drug or vehicle control (DMSO), lysed, labeled with TMT, and analyzed by LC-MS/MS.

### Data Protocol
Data was searched in Max Quant.  Perseus and GSEA analyses were performed.
