### Accession
PXD019704

### Title
Chaperone-mediated autophagy in the regulation of the circadian clock

### Description
Oscillatory lysosomal activity for disposal of the components of the cellular clock is essential to sustain organismal circadian rhythms

### Sample Protocol
Solubilization of purified lysosomes in 5% SDS/50mM TEAB. Trypsin digestion using solid phase columns(s-TRAP). RP-HPLC MS/MS analysis in Qexactive-HFX

### Data Protocol
Raw files were processed with MaxQuant (v 1.6.7.0) using the standard settings against a mouse protein database (UniProtKB/TrEMBL, 53449 sequences)
