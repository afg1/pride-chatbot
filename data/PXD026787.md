### Title
Mesenchymal stem cells from an inflammed area and normal tissue post anterior cruciate ligament injury

### Description
Mesenchymal stem cells from an inflammed area and a uninflammed tissue post anterior cruciate ligament injury

### Sample Protocol
MSCs were collected from patients and grown in a dish. Cells were lsyed and subjected to a quantitative shotgun proteomics workflow.

### Data Protocol
samples were analyzed using MaxQuant, Metascape and STRING-db.
