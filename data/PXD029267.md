### Title
The spread of herds and horses into the Altai

### Description
This project explores dietary proteins in human dental calculus through shotgun proteomics

### Sample Protocol
We used a single pot, solid phase sample extraction (SP3) modified for ancient human dental calculus. Digestion with trypsin and then peptides were measured via LC-MS/MS.

### Data Protocol
We analyzed the results with Mascot and used both Swissprot and a custon curated database of dairy proteins.
