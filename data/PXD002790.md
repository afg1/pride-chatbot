### Title
Systematic analysis of the human Nrf2 network

### Description
investigation of Nrf2 protein complexes and post-translational modification profiles under different methods of stimulation in SKNAS neuroblastoma cells

### Sample Protocol
whole cell lysis and immunoprecipitation, on-bead reduction and alkylation, in-gel digestion and peptide extraction.

### Data Protocol
Label free quantification using Andromeda and MaxQuant
