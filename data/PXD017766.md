### Title
Olfactory bulb, mid-age mice, Thon Collaboration

### Description
Proteome analysis of  mouse brain tissue samples from olfactory bulb of middle-aged (8-13 months) female mice.

### Sample Protocol
Samples were analyzed by Q-Exactive coupled directly to EASY 1000 nLC. The protein lysates were in-solution digested on FASP with the enzyme trypsin.

### Data Protocol
nLC-MS/MS results were searched using MaxQuant (version 1.6.0.16) against the mus musculus proteome (uniprot ID: UP000000589).
