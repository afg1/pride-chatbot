### Title
Mutant calreticulin binders analysis

### Description
Mutant, truncated and wild-type human calreticul was co-purified with binding proteins

### Sample Protocol
Co-purified proteins were resolved by SDS-PAGE, in-gel digested using trypsin, and analyzed by LC-MS

### Data Protocol
MaxQuant was used to identify and quantify (label-free) proteins
