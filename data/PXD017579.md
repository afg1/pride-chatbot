### Title
EE1A1 Modifications

### Description
Analysis of eEF1A1 posttranslational modifications of Mocetinostat-treated and non-treated control cells

### Sample Protocol
IPs were separated by SDS-PAGE, the area in which eEF1A1 should run was excised and proteins in-gel digested with trypsin.

### Data Protocol
Data were processed by MaxQuant version 1.6.2.10 using a full-length mouse Uniprot databas (version April 2016).
