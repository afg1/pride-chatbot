### Title
Proteomic analysis of the vitreous body in PDR and NPDR

### Description
Proteomic analysis of the vitreous body in PDR and NPDR to determine potential biomarkers.

### Sample Protocol
Samples were digested using in-solution method. Proteome digests were analyzed using the Thermo Q-Exactive LC-MS/MS system.

### Data Protocol
MS spectra were subjected to MaxQuant ver.1.5.8.3 for label free quantification, then analyzed using Perseus 1.6.5.0.
