### Title
Interactions between basic helix-loop-helix transcription factors and nucleosomes

### Description
In solution crosslinking mass spectrometry of transcription factors in complex with nucleosomes

### Sample Protocol
DSSO-based crosslinking of recombinant proteins and measurement by MS2/MS3 on a Thermo instrument as described in Michael et al.

### Data Protocol
XlinkX/Proteome Discoverer analysis using the default settings for crosslinking-MS as described in Michael et al.
