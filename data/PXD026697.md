### Title
Study of endogenous interactome of Myosin VI

### Description

### Sample Protocol
Cell lysis, IP of Myosin VI, in-gel protein digestion, nLC-ESI-MS analysis

### Data Protocol
Data analysis performed by Mascot Search Engine and Scaffold
