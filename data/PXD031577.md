### Title
Grad-seq Enterococcus faecalis and faecium

### Description
Grad-seq in Enterococcus faecalis V583 and Enterococcus faecium AUS004.

### Sample Protocol
Gradient fractions were solubilized in SDS-PAGE, reduced, acylated, precipitated and then in-solution digested for MS.

### Data Protocol
Data were analyzed by MaxQuant for the proteomes of E. faecalis = UP000001415 and for faecium embl-cds
