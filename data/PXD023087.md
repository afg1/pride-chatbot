### Title
Human adenocarcinoma cell line for phosphoproteomics

### Description
Bispecific anti-EGFR/EPHA2 antibody displays strong tumor-suppressing activity

### Sample Protocol
MDA cell lines were grown in SILAC media then TiO2 enriched for phosphopeptides.

### Data Protocol
The peptides were run on Bruker Impact II Qtof, searched on MaxQuant 1.5.3.30 with default parameters, plus phosphorylation modification
