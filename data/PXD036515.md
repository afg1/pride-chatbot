### Accession
PXD036515

### Title
An open-label window of opportunity trial using Mifepristone for breast cancer patients with higher levels of progesterone receptor isoform A than isoform B (MIPRA)

### Description
A label-free analysis of breast cancer samples was performed

### Sample Protocol
Samples were lysed in urea and digested with Lys-C/trypsin using the standard FASP protocol. Peptides were analyzed by LC-MS/MS analysis using a Q Exactive HF-X

### Data Protocol
Raw files were analyzed with MaxQuant against a human protein database
