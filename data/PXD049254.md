### Title
Chemogentic heart failute: from adults to neonatal models of disease

### Description
The submission is related to the article which demonstrates that in utero generation of Hydrogen Peroxide in the developing heart induces significant systolic dysfunction upon birth.

### Sample Protocol
the whole cardiac tissue was minced and the proteins were isolated by treating the tissue with RIPA buffer. Total protein was then precipitated with TCAand resolubilized in Rapigest SF.Resolubilized proteins were treated with sequencing grade Trypsin at 37 degrees overnight.Then, sample was run through LTQ orbitrap Velos Eliteion trap mass spectrometer.

### Data Protocol
Initial data analysis and annotation ws performed by Sequest. Downstream data analysis was done by DAVID and different R packages.
