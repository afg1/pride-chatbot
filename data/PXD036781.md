### Title
A Natural Biological Adhesive from Snail Mucus for Wound Repair

### Description
The information of main proteins from the two d-SMGs were identified

### Sample Protocol
Snail mucus proteins were extracted and digested without other purification; and the obtained peptides were analyzed and detected by Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry

### Data Protocol
The raw MS files were analyzed and searched against the protein database based on Stylommatophora, a mutual taxonomic level for Achatina fulica and Helix lucorum (https://www.uniprot.org/, taxonomy_id:6527) by using Maxquant (1.6.2.10)
