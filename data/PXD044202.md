### Title
Discovery and characterization of a novel WRN inhibitor

### Description
Discovery and characterization of a novel WRN inhibitor

### Sample Protocol
Cell pellets of 16 samples (plus/minus WRN inhibitor) were lysed in urea, digested, TMT16-labeled, fractionated by High pH RP and analyzed on a LUMOS mass spec using an MS3 method

### Data Protocol
Data was analyzed using Proteome Discoverer followed by filtering, grouping and statistical analysis using Python scripts
