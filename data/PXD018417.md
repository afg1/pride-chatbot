### Title
Ovarian Cancer Biomarkers - DIGE Spot identification

### Description
Identification of markers for the diagnosis of early stage ovarian cancer

### Sample Protocol
Spots have been cut from 2D saturation DIGE gels, digested with trypsin and analysed via LC-MS/MS on an Impact HD Q-TOF MS

### Data Protocol
Data processing workflow scripts can be downloaded from https://github.com/medardus333/OvCa_Markers
