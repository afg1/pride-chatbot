### Title
Metagenome and Viral Interrogation of a Moose Rumen Microbial Community

### Description
Proteomic data from moose fistulated rumen; metagenomic and viral sequencing performed; metabolic pathways reconstructed

### Sample Protocol
Samples were digested with trypsin, then analyzed by LC-MS/MS

### Data Protocol
Data was searched with MSGF+ using PNNL's DMS Processing pipeline
