### Title
Evaluation of preservation quality and viability of FFPE ovarian cancer tissue samples

### Description
Analysis of 60 FFPE (formalin-fixed, paraffin-embedded) ovarian cancer tissue samples to evaluate preservation quality and viability of global and phosphoproteomic analysis

### Sample Protocol
Samples were digested with trypsin, then analyzed by LC-MS/MS

### Data Protocol
Data was searched with MSGF+ using PNNL's DMS Processing pipeline
